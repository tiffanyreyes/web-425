Antonio Vivaldi


Antonio Vivaldi was an Italian composer whose music primarily focused on the Baroque era. He was a violinist so most compositions had a preference for the instrument, though he also wrote many operas and choral work. He composed many concertos for a variety of instruments but he is most remembered for his violin concertos, the most famous on being the Four Seasons.
